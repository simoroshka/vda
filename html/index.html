Graph editing mode

  • Double click on the canvas to add new vertex.
  • Double click on a vertex to start new edge. Click once on another vertex to connect the edge.
  • Right click to delete vertices or edges.
  • Drag a vertex to move it.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • Shift - graph editing mode.
  • T - place tokens randomly.
  • P - play/pause.
